Our Story

A Legacy of
Culinary Excellence

Founded in 2008 by Chef Antoine Moreau, Lumière was born from a single conviction: that dining is theatre, and every dish a carefully crafted scene. Our kitchen draws from the classical French tradition while embracing the finest local produce.

Fifteen years later, across two continents, we remain committed to the same founding principle — that food, when given intention, becomes an experience that transcends the meal itself.

03 — Experience

Every Detail Considered

I
Private Dining
Our exclusive private room seats up to 14 guests, perfect for corporate events, celebrations, and intimate gatherings with bespoke menus crafted to your occasion.
II
Wine Cellar Tours
Descend into our 2,000-bottle cellar with our sommelier for an immersive tour and private tasting of our most extraordinary labels from Burgundy to Barossa.
III
Chef's Table
Six seats at the kitchen counter. Watch the brigade at work as Chef Moreau guides you through a 12-course tasting menu created exclusively for that evening.
IV
Cooking Masterclass
Spend a morning in our kitchen learning foundational French techniques, the art of sauce, and the philosophy behind seasonal cuisine. Available on select Saturdays.
V
Sunday Brunch
A languid, unhurried affair. Free-flowing Champagne, a market-table of charcuterie, and a rotating menu celebrating the week's finest produce. Sundays 11am–3pm.
VI
Gift Experiences
Give the gift of Lumière. Our beautifully packaged experience vouchers are available for dining, wine tastings, and cooking masterclasses — presented in hand-finished boxes.
